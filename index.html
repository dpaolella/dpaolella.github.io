Altair D3

An example interactive plot made using Altair

This data visualization shows 2014 per capita CO2 emissions and population growth rates (averaged over the past 5 years) by country.[1] Of particular interest are the many African countries that have low per capita emissions and high population growth rates. If there were modest increases in per capita CO2 emissions in these countries (similar to levels in developing countries on other continents), combined with rapidly growing populations, there would be a substantial increase in global emissions.

[1] Data from The World Bank

Interactive features:

  • By clicking and dragging on the scatter plot, you can highlight points and automatically filter the data displayed in the histogram below. Click on empty space in the scatter plot to undo your selection.
  • By clicking on a bar in the histogram, you can filter the data displayed on the scatter plot. Click on empty space in the histogram to undo your selection.
